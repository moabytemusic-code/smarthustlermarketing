# Product Specification: Micro Niche Finder AI

## 1. Product Overview
*   **Name:** Micro Niche Finder AI
*   **Tagline:** Validate profitable markets in seconds.
*   **Price:** $27 (One-time)
*   **Format:** Digital Tool (Prompt Pack) + Video Workshop
*   **Target Audience:** Aspiring entrepreneurs stuck in "Analysis Paralysis" who don't know what to build.

## 2. The Problem
Most people fail because they pick niches that are:
1.  **Too Broad:** "Fitness" (You will die competing with Nike).
2.  **Too Poor:** "College Students" (They have no money).
3.  **Too Crowded:** "Make Money Online" (Sharks everywhere).

## 3. The Solution
A proprietary "AI Chain" (series of prompts) that forces ChatGPT/Claude to:
1.  **Scan** real-time market data (if enabled) or behavioral patterns.
2.  **Identify** "Bleeding Neck" pain points.
3.  **Calculate** the "Profitability Score" (0-100) based on purchase power and competition.
4.  **Generate** the "Asset Plan" (What specifically to build).

## 4. Deliverables (The Bundle)
*   **The Master Prompt:** A 500-word "Mega-Prompt" that instructs AI to act as a 20-year Market Research veteran.
*   **The Validation Checklist:** A PDF scorecard to manually verify the AI's output.
*   **The "Niche Sniper" Video:** A 20-minute over-the-shoulder video of YOU finding 3 profitable niches live.
*   **Bonus:** "The Anti-Niche List" (50 niches to avoid at all costs).

## 5. Sales Copy Angles (Fascinations)
*   "How to know if a niche will pay before you write a single word."
*   "The 'Reddit Hack' that reveals exactly what people are begging to buy."
*   "Why following your 'passion' is the fastest way to stay broke (and what to follow instead)."

## 6. Website Integration
*   **Image:** `/images/products/micro-niche-finder.png` (Futuristic glowing radar/target interface).
*   **Category:** "Systems" or "Tools".
*   **Features:**
    *   One-Click Validation
    *   Profitability Scorer
    *   Competition Radar
