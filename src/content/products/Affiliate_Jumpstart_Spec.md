# Product Specification: The Affiliate Jumpstart

## 1. Product Overview
*   **Name:** The Affiliate Jumpstart
*   **Tagline:** Make your first $1,000 without creating a product.
*   **Price:** $97 (One-time)
*   **Format:** Deep-Dive Workshop + Asset Templates
*   **Target Audience:** Creators who have an audience but nothing to sell, or beginners who don't want to build a product yet.

## 2. The Problem
Most people think affiliate marketing is spamming links on Twitter.
*   **Reality:** Real affiliate income comes from building "Asset Pages" (Comparison grids, Review Hubs, Tutorials).
*   **Pain:** "I don't know how to sell without being salesy."

## 3. The Solution
A complete blueprint for "Asset-Based Affiliate Marketing".
Instead of interrupting people, you build resources they search for.
It includes:
1.  **The "Review Hub" Template:** A Notion/Web page structure that converts at 5%+.
2.  **The Comparison Grid:** "Category King" vs "Budget Option" frameworks.
3.  **Email Sequences:** The "Bridge Sequence" to warm up cold traffic before sending them to an offer.
4.  **Partner List:** A curated list of 50 High-Ticket Affiliate Programs (Recurring Commissions).

## 4. Deliverables
*   **Workshop:** 90-Minute implementation session.
*   **Templates:** Copy-paste HTML/Notion templates for review pages.
*   **The "Black Book" of Partners:** PDF directory of high-paying software programs.

## 5. Sales Copy Angles
*   "Don't build the product. Own the distribution."
*   "The lazy man's way to software margins."
*   "How to get paid for recommending tools you already use."
