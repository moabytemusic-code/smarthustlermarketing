# Product Specification: The Viral Content Engine

## 1. Product Overview
*   **Name:** The Viral Content Engine
*   **Tagline:** A Notion System to produce 30 days of content in 3 hours.
*   **Price:** $47 (One-time)
*   **Format:** Notion Template + SOP Library
*   **Target Audience:** Solopreneurs who are inconsistent with posting because their process is messy.

## 2. The Problem
Content creation fails due to friction:
*   "I don't know what to write." (No Idea Bank)
*   "I forgot to post." (No Scheduler)
*   "I lost that file." (No Asset Library)

## 3. The Solution
A simplified version of the Founder's OS, specifically carved out for Content Creators.
It includes:
1.  **The Idea Capture System:** A frictionless way to dump ideas on mobile.
2.  **The Production Kanban:** Status tracking (Idea -> Draft -> Edit -> Scheduled).
3.  **The Repurposing Matrix:** How to turn 1 Tweet into a Blog, a LinkedIn Post, and a Short.
4.  **Platform Templates:** Pre-formatted blocks for X, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

## 4. Deliverables
*   **Notion Template Link:** One-click clone.
*   **SOP: The "Batching" Method:** Video guide on how to batch produce.
*   **Hook Library:** 50 Viral Hooks for B2B.

## 5. Sales Copy Angles

*   "Stop staring at a blank cursor."
*   "Treat your media like a media company."
