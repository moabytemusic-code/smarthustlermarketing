# Bing's AI Citations: Unlock GEO Goldmine

Microsoft's new **AI Performance dashboard** in Bing Webmaster Tools lets businesses track content citations in Copilot and Bing AI answers, filling a critical visibility gap in the AI search era.[1][2][4]

## The Situation

Bing launched the **AI Performance dashboard** in public preview on February 9, 2026, providing the first dedicated tool for measuring how often your site's content is cited in AI-generated responses across Copilot, Bing AI summaries, and partner integrations.[1][2][6][8] This addresses a long-standing blind spot: unlike traditional SEO metrics like clicks or rankings, AI citations reveal true generative visibility without requiring traffic.[4][5]

Key metrics include **total citations** (overall references in AI answers), **average cited pages** (daily unique URLs cited), **grounding queries** (exact phrases AI uses to retrieve your content), **page-level activity** (top-performing pages), and **visibility trends** over time.[1][2][3]

Unlike Google Search Console, which lumps AI Overviews into general performance without page-level or query breakdowns, Bing offers granular, actionable data.[1][4]

## The Breakdown

- **Total Citations**: Counts how frequently your site sources AI answers in a given period, signaling authority in generative search.[2][5]
- **Average Cited Pages**: Tracks daily unique page references, showing content breadth.[1][3]
- **Grounding Queries**: Reveals trigger phrases (e.g., specific questions AI matches to your content), enabling precise optimization.[1][2][4]
- **Page-Level Data**: Identifies high-citation URLs and trends, highlighting winners and optimization opportunities.[3][6]
- **Trends**: Visualizes citation fluctuations, correlating with content updates or events.[1][7]

Industry experts praise it as a GEO breakthrough, with Bing outperforming Google in transparency. IndexNow integration accelerates this by pushing fresh content to AI faster.[3][6] Social reactions note frustration that Google lags, but Bing sets the standard.[4]

## Why This Matters

For **business owners and marketers**, AI citations build **brand authority** without clicks—users see your site referenced in AI responses, driving indirect trust and traffic.[5] In a post-ranking world, GEO prioritizes being "cited as a source" over position 1, shifting strategies to structured, evidence-based content with headings, tables, and FAQs.[3][5]

Entrepreneurs gain free, real-time signals to refine content, report ROI on AI visibility, and outpace competitors still guessing. As AI dominates search, untracked citations mean invisible opportunities; this dashboard turns them into strategic assets.[4][5]

## Action Plan

1. **Verify Bing Webmaster Tools**: Sign up, submit your sitemap, and locate the AI Performance dashboard under public preview.[1][8]
2. **Analyze Metrics**: Review total citations, top pages, and grounding queries to identify high-performers and query patterns.[2][3]
3. **Optimize Top Content**: Enhance cited pages with structured data, fresh stats, FAQs, and tables; test grounding query matches.[3][6]
4. **Implement IndexNow**: Integrate for instant content updates, boosting AI recrawls and citation speed.[3][6]
5. **Monitor Trends Weekly**: Track visibility changes, A/B test updates, and correlate with analytics for full ROI.[1][7]
