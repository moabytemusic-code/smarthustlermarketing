# 90-Day Local SEO Plan: Boost Visibility Fast

## The Situation

A new webinar from Search Engine Journal outlines a **90-day plan** to dramatically improve **local search visibility** for businesses with multiple locations. Titled "90 Days. 1 Plan. Improved Local Search Visibility," it focuses on practical steps to enhance rankings across each site in your network while preparing your brand for **AI-driven search** dominance[1][5]. This comes at a critical time as **generative AI** reshapes local queries, prioritizing hyper-local, authoritative content over generic listings[1][4][8].

## The Breakdown

The plan emphasizes three core pillars backed by 2026 local SEO best practices:
- **Optimize Google Business Profiles (GBP):** Fully complete profiles with accurate categories, fresh photos, regular posts, and **E-E-A-T signals** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) to secure the **Google 3-Pack**—high-converting spots above organic results[1][5].
- **Build Geo-Modified Pages:** Create dedicated pages for each service area with **location-specific keywords** in titles, H1s, URLs, embedded maps, local testimonials, landmarks, and **FAQ schema** to target "near me" and implicit local searches[1][2][4].
- **Leverage Social and Citations:** Maintain **NAP consistency** (Name, Address, Phone) across directories like Yelp and Bing, engage authentically on social media, and produce hyper-local content for **featured snippets** and **AI overviews**[1][3][5].

Stats show optimized GBPs drive direction requests and calls, while geo-pages boost AI citations by addressing commercial questions like "best plumber in [city]"[2][5]. Recoveries in rankings often occur within **60-90 days** with consistent effort[5].

## Why This Matters

For business owners and marketers managing multi-location networks, this plan counters AI's shift toward zero-click answers and agentic search, where visibility hinges on **local relevance** and **snippet-worthy content**[5][7][8]. Poor local SEO means missing 46% of searches that are local, leading to lost revenue from high-intent "near me" traffic[1]. In 2026, brands ignoring AI optimization risk invisibility as tools like Google Maps and AI overviews favor active, trusted profiles[5][8]. This directly impacts lead generation, with **3-Pack** spots converting far higher than organic results[1].

## Action Plan

Implement this **90-day roadmap** for measurable gains:
1. **Days 1-30: Audit and Optimize GBP** – Claim/verify profiles, add services/photos/posts, ensure NAP consistency across 50+ directories, and monitor via Google Search Console[1][2][5].
2. **Days 31-60: Build Geo-Pages** – Develop 5-10 location-specific pages with embedded maps, local FAQs (using question-based H2s), testimonials, and schema markup for AI snippets[1][4].
3. **Days 61-90: Amplify with Content and Social** – Publish hyper-local blogs/case studies, engage on social for signals, track impressions/clicks, and refine based on performance data[1][3][5].
4. **Ongoing: Review Velocity** – Solicit fresh reviews, update Q&A, and use **BLUF** (Bottom Line Up Front) summaries to capture AI overviews[1][5].
5. **Measure Success** – Target 20-50% impression growth, monitor calls/directions, and aim for 3-Pack dominance[2][5].
