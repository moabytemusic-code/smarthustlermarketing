# Google's Meridian Scenario Planner: No-Code MMM for Smarter Budgets

## The Situation

Google has launched **Scenario Planner**, a no-code tool integrated with its open-source **Meridian** Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) platform. This update allows marketers to access historical performance data from channels like Google, Meta, and LinkedIn, test budget scenarios, and receive real-time **ROI estimates** without coding or data science expertise[1][2][4]. Originally tested in March 2024 and released in January 2025, Meridian now becomes truly accessible via this intuitive interface[1][5]. Meanwhile, Microsoft's Performance Max training path offers hands-on updates for PPC optimization[initial snippet].

## The Breakdown

**Meridian** uses advanced AI like gradient-boosting regression trees and Bayesian causal inference to analyze large-scale data, predict campaign impacts, and optimize budgets across channels[1][5][6]. Scenario Planner transforms complex MMM outputs into visual simulations: adjust budgets, stress-test strategies, and forecast ROI in real-time[2][3][4]. Key features include:
- Pulling multi-channel historical data for pattern identification.
- Hypothetical scenario testing (e.g., "What if I double YouTube spend?")[6].
- Frequency optimization and model fit reporting[5].

Limitations persist: models require training on historical data, and advanced interpretation may still need analytics skills[1]. Harvard Business Review notes nearly **40% of organizations** struggle to action MMM insights—Scenario Planner bridges this gap[2]. Real-world cases show up to **40% revenue growth** from optimized Google ad investments[8].

## Why This Matters

For business owners and marketers, this democratizes MMM, eliminating reliance on expensive data scientists. Test allocations across channels to maximize every dollar, adapt to privacy changes (no cookies needed), and shift from reactive reporting to proactive planning[3][6][7]. In competitive PPC landscapes, tools like this deliver precise incrementality, elasticity, and channel contributions—directly boosting bottom-line ROI amid economic shifts[5][8].

## Action Plan

1. **Train Your Meridian Model**: Upload historical data from Google, Meta, and other channels to fit the MMM—leverage geo-level data for accuracy[1][5].
2. **Access Scenario Planner**: Use the no-code UI to simulate budget shifts and view instant ROI projections[2][4].
3. **Stress-Test Strategies**: Run 'what-if' scenarios (e.g., reallocate 20% from TV to Search) and identify optimal frequencies[5][6].
4. **Integrate Performance Max Training**: Combine with Microsoft's hands-on path for Google Ads mastery[initial snippet].
5. **Monitor and Iterate**: Evaluate model fit stats and calibrate with experiments for ongoing optimization[5].
