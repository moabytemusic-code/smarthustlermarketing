**Goal:** Click Through Rate (CTR) is king. Copy these proven layouts.

## Template 1: The "Before vs. After" (Split Screen)
*   **Visual:** Split the image down the middle.
*   **Left Side (Red tint):** Sad person, empty wallet, bad result. Text: "FAIL".
*   **Right Side (Green tint):** Happy person, cash stack, great result. Text: "SUCCESS".
*   **Face:** Shocked expression in the middle.

## Template 2: The "Big Arrow"
*   **Visual:** A screenshot of a complex graph or dashboard.
*   **Element:** A HUGE red arrow pointing to one tiny specific detail.
*   **Text:** 3 words max. (e.g., "1,000% ROI" or "Don't Do This").
*   **Psychology:** Curiosity. "What is the arrow pointing at?"

## Template 3: The "Negative Space"
*   **Visual:** Solid bright background (Yellow or Bright Blue).
*   **Element:** Cutout of you holding one specific object (Phone, Money, Camera).
*   **Text:** Big bold question. "Is it Dead?" or "Scam?"
*   **Psychology:** Clean, stands out against cluttered feeds.

## Template 4: The "Listicle"
*   **Visual:** You holding up 3 fingers.
*   **Text (Left):** "3 Tools"
*   **Text (Right):** Icons of the tools (ChatGPT logo, Canva logo, etc).
*   **Psychology:** "I want to know what the tools are."

## Template 5: The "Proof"
*   **Visual:** Close up of a notification on a phone screen.
*   **Detail:** "Stripe: You received $500.00".
*   **Background:** Blurred city street.
*   **Text:** "It works."
