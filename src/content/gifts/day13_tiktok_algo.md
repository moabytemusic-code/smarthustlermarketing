**Goal:** Understand how the "For You Page" (FYP) actually works so you can exploit it.

## The Metrics That Matter (In Order)
The algorithm scores your video based on points. Highest score wins.

1.  **Retention (Watch Time):** (10 Points). Did they watch until the end?
2.  **Rewatches:** (8 Points). Did they loop it?
3.  **Shares:** (6 Points). Did they send it to a friend? (High viral signal).
4.  **Comments:** (4 Points). Did it start a conversation?
5.  **Likes:** (1 Point). Vanity metric. Doesn't matter much.

## The Hierarchy of Distribution
How TikTok tests your content:

1.  **Batch 1 (200-500 views):** Shown to a mix of followers and randoms.
    *   *Goal:* Get 15% retention.
2.  **Batch 2 (1,000-5,000 views):** Shown to people interested in your tags/hashtags.
    *   *Goal:* Get engagement (Shares/Comments).
3.  **Batch 3 (10,000-100,000+ views):** Wide distribution.
    *   *Goal:* Broad appeal.

## 3 Hacks to Boost Your Score today
1.  **The "Loop" Hack:** Make the end of your sentence connect to the start of the video.
    *   *End:* "...and that is the secret to..."
    *   *Start:* "Making money online."
2.  **The "Argument" Bait:** Intentionally leave a small, harmless mistake (typo or mispronunciation). People will comment to correct you.
3.  **The "Saveable" List:** Put a fast-moving list on screen for 2 seconds. People have to re-watch and pause to read it.
