**Goal:** Turn your boring life into a compelling story.
**Based on:** Joseph Campbell's Monomyth.

## 1. The Ordinary World
Where were you before? (The struggle).
*   *My Story:* I was working 60 hours a week at a job I hated. I had $42 in my bank account.

## 2. The Call to Adventure
What triggered the change?
*   *My Story:* I saw a guy on YouTube making money with a laptop. I decided I had to figure it out.

## 3. The Refusal of the Call
The fear/doubt.
*   *My Story:* I thought it was a scam. I told myself "I'm not tech savvy."

## 4. Meeting the Mentor
The guide/tool that helped.
*   *My Story:* I found a course called [Product Name].

## 5. Crossing the Threshold
Taking action.
*   *My Story:* I bought the course. I stayed up for 3 nights straight building my funnel.

## 6. The Ordeal
The failure before success.
*   *My Story:* My first ad account got banned. I cried.

## 7. The Reward
The First Win.
*   *My Story:* Then, on Tuesday morning, I got a notification: "You made a sale!"

## 8. The Return
Coming back to help others.
*   *My Story:* Now, I teach people exactly how I did it.
