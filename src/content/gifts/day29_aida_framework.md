**Goal:** The universal formula for writing persuasive copy.

## A - Attention
**Goal:** Stop the scroll.
**How:** A bold headline, a shocking statement, or a question.
*   *Example:* "Stop drinking coffee first thing in the morning."

## I - Interest
**Goal:** Keep them reading.
**How:** Facts, stats, or empathy.
*   *Example:* "Did you know that caffeine blocks cortisol production? It actually makes you more tired at 2pm."

## D - Desire
**Goal:** Make them want the solution.
**How:** Paint a picture of the "After" state (Benefits).
*   *Example:* "Imagine waking up with natural energy, no jitters, and no afternoon crash."

## A - Action
**Goal:** Tell them exactly what to do.
**How:** A clear, direct command.
*   *Example:* "Click here to try our decaf blend."
