# The Zero-Ad-Spend Traffic Checklist
## Get your first 100 visitors without spending a dollar

### ‚úÖ WEEK 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

**Reddit Strategy**
- [ ] Find 5 relevant subreddits for your niche
- [ ] Read the rules (avoid instant bans)
- [ ] Comment helpfully on 10 posts (build karma)
- [ ] Post your "I built this" story with link
- [ ] Reply to every comment within 1 hour

**X/Twitter Strategy**
- [ ] Follow 50 people in your niche
- [ ] Post 3 tweets about your building journey
- [ ] Use 3-5 relevant hashtags per tweet
- [ ] Reply to 10 tweets from bigger accounts
- [ ] Share a "behind the scenes" thread

**Hacker News (if tech-related)**
- [ ] Post on "Show HN" between 8-10am PST
- [ ] Title format: "Show HN: [What it does] ([tech stack])"
- [ ] Reply to every comment
- [ ] Cross-post to relevant subreddits

---

### ‚úÖ WEEK 2: Amplification (Days 8-14)

**Content Repurposing**
- [ ] Turn your landing page into a blog post
- [ ] Create 5 TikTok/Reels from your product demo
- [ ] Write a LinkedIn post about "why I built this"
- [ ] Submit to Product Hunt (Thursday launch)
- [ ] Post in 3 Facebook groups (value-first)

**Direct Outreach**
- [ ] Find 10 potential users on Twitter
- [ ] DM them: "Hey, I saw you're into [niche]. Built something you might like"
- [ ] Offer free lifetime access for feedback
- [ ] Ask for a testimonial if they like it

**Community Engagement**
- [ ] Answer 5 Quora questions in your niche
- [ ] Link to your tool as "I actually built this for..."
- [ ] Join 2 Discord servers related to your niche
- [ ] Be helpful first, promote second

---

### ‚úÖ ONGOING: The Compound Effect

**Daily Habits (15 mins/day)**
- [ ] Post 1 update on Twitter
- [ ] Reply to 5 comments/DMs
- [ ] Check Reddit for new "help" posts
- [ ] Share one user win/testimonial

**Weekly Habits (1 hour/week)**
- [ ] Write 1 blog post or case study
- [ ] Create 3 short-form videos
- [ ] Reach out to 5 new potential users
- [ ] Update your Product Hunt page

### üí° Pro Tips

1. **The "Helpful First" Rule**: Answer 80% of their question in your comment, then link to your tool for the remaining 20%
