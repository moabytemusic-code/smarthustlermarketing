# Micro Niche Finder AI (Prompt Library)

**Status:** Premium Asset
**License:** Personal Use Only

## Overview
Use these prompts with ChatGPT (GPT-4 recommended) or Claude to validate business ideas in seconds.

## The Prompts

### 1. The "Blue Ocean" Scanner
> "Act as a market researcher. I am interested in the [INDUSTRY] space. List 10 specific 'micro-niches' within this industry that are currently underserved but have high purchasing power. For each, estimate the 'Pain Point Intensity' on a scale of 1-10."

### 2. The Profitability Validator
> "Analyze the following niche: [INSERT NICHE]. Identify 3 potential high-ticket products ($500+) and 3 low-ticket recurring products ($20-50/mo) that could solve problems for this audience. Estimate the 'Time to MVP' for each."

### 3. The Competitor Radar
> "Who are the top 3 incumbents in [INSERT NICHE]? What are their biggest negative reviews complaining about? Summarize the gap in the market."

## Workflow
1.  Run Prompt 1 to get ideas.
2.  Pick your top 3.
3.  Run Prompt 2 to see how you'd make money.
4.  Run Prompt 3 to see if you can beat the current players.
