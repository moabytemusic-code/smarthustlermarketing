**Status:** Premium Asset

## Overview
A complete set of scripts and templates to close deals ranging from $2k to $10k without feeling "salesy."

## Contents

### 1. The "Diagnosis" Sales Script
*   **Phase 1:** The Gap (Where are they vs. Where they want to be)
*   **Phase 2:** The Cost of Inaction (What happens if they do nothing?)
*   **Phase 3:** The Bridge (How your offer solves it)
*   **Phase 4:** The Investment (Price anchor + value stack)
