## üèé Module 1: The "Mega-Prompt" Framework

Stop writing one-sentence requests. Use this structure for 10x better results.

**1. Role**: "Act as an expert copywriter with 10 years of experience in direct response..."
**2. Context**: "I am writing a blog post for beginner affiliate marketers who are skeptical of gurus..."
**3. Task**: "Write a 1,500 word comprehensive guide on [Topic]."
**4. Constraints**: "Use short sentences. Avoid jargon. Use the word 'you' frequently. Do not use the words 'delve', 'explore', or 'landscape'."
**5. Format**: "Output in Markdown with H2 and H3 headers."

## üìù Module 2: The Blog Post Factory

### Phase 1: The Outline
*Prompt:*
"Generate 5 unique angles for a blog post about [Topic]. For each angle, provide a provocative headline and 3 bullet points."

### Phase 2: The Drafting (Section by Section)
*Do not ask for the whole post at once. AI gets lazy.*

*Prompt:*
"Write the Introduction for Angle #3. Start with a shocking statistic or a relatable story. Hook the reader in the first 50 words."

*Prompt (Follow-up):*
"Now write the section for 'Key Point 1'. Include a table comparing X vs Y."

### Phase 3: The Polish
*Prompt:*
"Rewrite the following text to sound more human. Vary sentence length. Add 2 analogies. Lower the reading level to Grade 8."

## üìπ Module 3: Instant Video Scripts

### The Viral Hook Generator
*Prompt:*
"Write 10 viral hooks for a TikTok video about [Topic]. Format them as:
1. Negative Hook (Stop doing X...)
2. Curiosity Hook (The secret to X...)
3. Listicle Hook (3 tools for X...)"

### The 60-Second Script
*Prompt:*
"Write a 60-second video script based on Hook #3.
- 0-3s: The Hook
- 3-45s: The Value/Education (Fast paced)
- 45-60s: Call to Action (Follow for more)"
